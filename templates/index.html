The College Scorecard is a federal website that allows users to find, select, and compare higher-education institutions based on a few provided variables. While the website has its benefits, we found that the interface did not take advantage of the extensive dataset collected on schools, and lacked the detailed reasons users would pick certain schools. We decided that it would be an interesting project to leverage the college scorecard dataset and cover over 7000 higher education institutions by focusing on institutional, academic, admission, financial, and future value data. Combining all of this, we want to present an addendum to the college scorecard tool for prospective students to look beyond opaque college rankings to evaluate and compare particular colleges.
